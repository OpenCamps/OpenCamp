Open Camps
@ UN July 8 to 17, 2016

Open Camps will be the largest mission-driven open source conference in the world, with 6,000+ attendees joining for 25+ conferences & events, featuring 400+ sessions on dozens of open source technologies.

Partnership Options

We are striving to deliver excellent value to all Open Camps partners.

We will help you tailor a partnership package to give you the best possible exposure for your budget.

Please contact us via e-mail at partnerships@opencamps.org or phone at 212 203 8052.

Founding Partner

$25,000

Ideal for partners seeking the largest presence in the Partner Pavilion, a designated section in Labs Rooms, and full day use of a Labs room.

This is a one-time partnership option, and will provide for lifetime designation as a "Founding Partner" in relation to partnerships in future years, as well as a 10% discount on future partnerships until Open Camps 2020.

Founding Partner benefits include:

  • Table/Staff: 5+ team members and large 96" table in Partner Pavilion, 50" LCD provided
  • Labs/Demo Room: designated section in shared labs & platforms room with branding/signage, and ability to use a Lab Room for 1 day for developer workshops/tutorials (limited slots available)
  • Banners: 3 pop-up banners for organization at table in Partner Pavilion
  • Materials: printed materials at table in Partner Pavilion
  • Website: profile page on website, xx-large logo on partner page
  • Social: over a dozen thank you messages on social media
  • Program: xxx-large logo on conference program
  • Signage: xxx-large logo on combined signage
  • Job Fair: participation in Job Fair and receipt of all submitted resumes

  • Labs/Demo Room: designated section/tables in shared labs & platforms room, and ability to use a lab room for 1/2 of developer workshops/tutorials (limited slots available)
  • Banners: 3 pop-up banners for organization at table in Partner Pavilion
  • Materials: printed materials at table in Partner Pavilion
  • Website: profile page on website, xx-large logo on partner page
  • Social: 10 or more thank you messages on social media
  • Program: xx-large logo on conference program
  • Signage: xx-large logo on combined signage
  • Job Fair: participation in Job Fair and receipt of all submitted resumes

Bronze Partner

$2,500

Ideal for Partners that want a presence onsite during Open Camps, bo no staffed table in the Partner Pavilion.
